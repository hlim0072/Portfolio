## Malaysia House Rental Pricing
#### Unit: ADS1001 (2024, Semester 1)

Objective:
- Predict house rental pricing and identify predictive factors of rental price in Kuala Lumpur & Selangor areas in 2024.

Findings:
- <i>Completion year, Number of Rooms and Bathrooms, Parking availability, Size of rental house, Number of facilities</i>, and <i>Distance from urban centre</i> were the most significant predictors
